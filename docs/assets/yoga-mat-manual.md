## 🧘‍♀️ How to Use Your Mat

### 1\. Unrolling Your Mat

When you first unroll your Yoga Mat Pro, you may notice a slight curl at the edges due to packaging.

  * **To flatten:** Lay the mat flat on the ground with the curled edges facing down. You can place a heavy book on the corners for a few hours, or simply start practicing—your body weight will flatten it out quickly.

### 2\. Identifying the Top and Bottom

  * **Top Side:** Features a fine, grippy pattern designed for your hands and feet.
  * **Bottom Side:** Features a wavy, ridged pattern designed to grip the floor.

### 3\. Using the Carrying Strap

Your mat comes with a versatile carrying strap that doubles as a yoga strap for stretching.

1.  Roll your mat up tightly.
2.  Loosen the loops at both ends of the strap.
3.  Slip one loop over each end of the rolled mat.
4.  Pull the strap by the center handle to tighten the loops securely around the mat.

## 🧼 Care & Maintenance

To keep your Yoga Mat Pro in pristine condition, please follow these care guidelines:

**Do's:**

  * **Wipe Down After Use:** After a sweaty session, wipe the mat down with a damp cloth. For a deeper clean, use a mixture of water and a few drops of mild dish soap or a dedicated natural yoga mat cleaner.
  * **Air Dry Completely:** Always let the mat air dry flat or hanging over a shower rod before rolling it up.
  * **Roll Loosely:** Roll the mat with the top side (the side you practice on) facing *outward*. This prevents the corners from curling up during your next practice.

**Don'ts:**

  * **No Direct Sunlight:** Prolonged exposure to direct sunlight or extreme heat (like the trunk of a hot car) can cause the TPE material to degrade, warp, or lose its grip.
  * **No Washing Machines:** Never put your mat in a washing machine or dryer.
  * **Avoid Harsh Chemicals:** Do not use bleach, alcohol-based cleaners, or harsh essential oils directly on the mat, as they can break down the material.
  * **Keep Pets Away:** Cats and dogs may scratch or puncture the soft TPE foam with their claws.
